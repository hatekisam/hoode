Why exchange BNB to BTC?

BNB is a well-known crypto asset due to the fact that it was launched by Binance Exchange, the largest crypto exchange platform. Initially, it was introduced as a utility token for the exchange platform but with time it has obtained more use cases. BNB BEP2 is mostly used to pay trading fees on Binance and can be used to pay for transaction fees on the BNB Beacon Chain and Smart Chain. Also, you can use BNB to pay for online services, financial services, travel bookings and even to create smart contracts.

Since the day Bitcoin was introduced to the world in 2009, it has grown by an impressive amount and remains the top cryptocurrency on the market. It is a flagship of the blockchain and cryptocurrency world, so having some BTC in your wallet is always a good option. Even though smaller payments are sometimes less convenient with Bitcoin, it is still one of the most widely accepted cryptocurrencies by merchants, you can even pay for a Microsoft subscription with it.

Both assets are widely applicable in their own way and deserve a spot in the portfolio of every crypto user. However, it is not only about diversifying your portfolio but also expanding your opportunities in the world of crypto.
