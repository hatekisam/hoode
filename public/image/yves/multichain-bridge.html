How to set up a bridge solution

Submit an application
Using our contact form. We will reach out to you within 2 business days.
Follow the integration
Your personal manager will guide you through the integration process and answer all of your questions.
Launch your solution
Receive your tailored solution and share it with your community. The bridge will be accessible to all ChangeNOW partners and users.

Cases we serve best

Network Migration
Enable seamless migration to a new network for your customers, free of any service fees.
Multiple network support
Make your asset’s networks interexchangeable with no extra costs. Add pairs for over 850 coins for each of your token’s networks.
Mainnet launch
Empower your users to invest in your Mainnet coin, even without centralized exchange listings. A token is all we need to build a custom bridge.
Token Generation Event
Launch your brand-new asset and manage pre-sales using our cost-free custom swap solution.
Staking
Implement staking for any coin of your choice and pay out rewards to your customers in your token.
Personalized approach
Our bridge solution is designed to fit your project's specific needs and preferences. We are open to adjustments and customizations.
