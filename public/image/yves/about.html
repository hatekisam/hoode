About Us

ChangeNOW is a non-custodial service created for simple and fast cryptocurrency exchanges. We strive for maximum safety, simplicity, and convenience. We do not store your funds or require any sort of account creation.

ChangeNOW has almost 700 coins available for exchange and does not hold any limits; you can exchange as much as you want - account-free, worry-free, faster than light. The fiat option is also available - you can buy cryptocurrency with Visa or MasterCard through our third-party partner.
