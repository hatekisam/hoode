Token Q&A

NOW Token can be bought on cryptocurrency exchanges such as ChangeNOW, Coinbase DEX, and Uniswap.

NOW tokens fuel all the products within the NOW product ecosystem. You can get NOW Token as cashback in ChangeNOW and NOW Wallet. Also, you can get up to 12.5% ROI yearly by staking in NOW Wallet! Users can stake from 10 to 100,000 NOW tokens to earn up to 12.5% annually. Since ChangeNOW is a non-custodial exchange, its native ERC-20 NOW tokens are kept on a chain with no intermediary. Still, the longer a user holds the stake, the larger the reward! Moreover, the staking amount can be increased at any time without the multiplier being lost: it is completely up to the stakeholder how large the final bonus will be. Start staking NOW!

NOW Token is listed on several popular cryptocurrency exchanges, such as ChangeNOW and Uniswap. Also, we are listed in such wallets as NOW Wallet, Guarda, Trust, Zelcore, Trustee, Edge, and D‚ÄôCent.

The vesting plan for NOW Token involves releasing a certain percentage of tokens to team members and advisors over a period of time. You can learn more about it in WhitePaper. This helps to ensure that these tokens are not dumped on the market all at once, which could lead to a drop in price.
