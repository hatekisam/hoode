FAQ

Can I make a profit with ChangeNOW?
Yes, you can join our affiliate program and make 0.4% of the funds attracted by you. Here is the detailed instruction.
Where do I get my API key?
You can join our Partners Program by registering over at ChangeNOW at Partners Login; the login is your email or your API key. Feel free to send the API key over to us so that we can put you in the database as our partner!
Do you work with liquidity providers?
We work with a large number of liquidity providers and constantly work on adding new ones. Having several liquidity providers helps us provide the best rates available and support as many asset pairs as possible (including unique ones that are rare to find on other exchanges).
How is KYC done? How do you manage risky funds?
ChangeNOW‚Äôs AML/KYC procedure is supported by an automated risk prevention system.
If a transaction is marked suspicious by the system, the exchange is put on hold and the customer in question is then asked to confirm their identity.

ChangeNOW doesn‚Äôt support any money laundering or other illegal activities; therefore, the service doesn‚Äôt enter any business agreements with individuals or business entities involved or suspected in such activities.

Each KYC case is handled individually. If a certain client is eligible for the procedure since their transaction had been marked as suspicious, they will have a specific status update on the exchange page along with a ‚ÄúVerify‚Äù button; the button will lead to our KYC provider‚Äôs verification tool.

If you wish to know more about the KYC procedure, please consult our General FAQ: https://changenow.io/faq/kyc-aml-procedure

What coins/tokens are available on ChangeNOW?
The full list of supported assets can be found here: https://changenow.io/status-page, which can also update you on the status of different asset pairs if you plan on performing an exchange.

If you already use our API, you can also check out our API endpoint ‚ÄúAvailable currencies‚Äù:
https://api.changenow.io/v2/exchange/currencies?active=&flow;=standard&buy;=&sell;=

The endpoint informs you on the availability of different assets, as well as the networks of the assets and whether the asset is available for Fixed Rate and Fiat exchanges.

How can I list a coin or a token on ChangeNOW?
If you wish to add an asset for it to become available for your customers on ChangeNOW, please take into account the following requirements for asset listing:
  1. The asset you are suggesting needs to be listed on CoinMarketCap with trackable markets (we will kindly ask you to provide us with the full name of your asset, as well as its link on https://coinmarketcap.com/);
  2. The asset needs to be represented by one of our liquidity providers (we will check this during our analysis);
  3. For CEX support, the asset needs to have a BTC pairing; for DEX support, the asset needs to have an ETH/wETH or a BNB/wBNB pairing.
  4. The asset also needs to have a sufficient liquidity pool for exchange processing (we will check this during the initial analysis).

If the suggested asset meets the initial requirements, fill out the Token Listing Application form and we will get back to you in 2 business days.

How can I track the API status and coin‚Äôs availability?
Please use the service below for tracking the uptime of our public API, as well as the status of supported assets: https://status.changenow.io/

You can also subscribe to specific events you are interested in, so we can update you on their progress.

How can I track the status of a transaction?
Check out: https://changenow.io/status-page

It can track an exchange status solely with a transaction ID, as well as update you on the status of different coins and pairs available on ChangeNOW.

What are your fees?
ChangeNOW‚Äôs fees are already included in the estimate and there are no hidden fees.

For every swap, ChangeNOW finds the fastest and most user-profitable way to execute an exchange. It is crucial to us that the final amount you receive is as close to the estimate as possible. That's why we calculate all the possible fees for every transaction thoroughly and include them in the estimate.

If you wish to know more about all the fees in ChangeNOW transactions, please consult our general FAQ: https://changenow.io/faq/what-fees-are-there-during-an-exchange
